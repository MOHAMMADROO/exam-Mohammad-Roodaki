Sandwich making:

For making a sandwich , there are 3 rules as below :

1 : Each sandwich needs at least one kind of meat (beef , chicken or turkey).

2 : A sandwich has either beef or chicken, but not both at the same time.

3 : If the sandwich has turkey, it should has cheese as well.
